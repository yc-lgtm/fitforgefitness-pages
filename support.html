FitForgeFitness Support

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Getting Help

FitForgeFitness support is provided through the official support contact associated with the app listing. Use that contact path for help with bugs, billing, install issues, or general questions.

App Basics

FitForgeFitness stores workout plans, sessions, and progress locally on your device. You can export your data from Settings at any time.

Troubleshooting

  • Restart the app if timers or animations appear stuck.
  • Export a backup before clearing app data.
  • Use the latest iOS version supported by your device.
