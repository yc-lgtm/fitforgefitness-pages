FitForgeFitness Support

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Support Options

Need help with FitForgeFitness? Use the public support path below.

  • Open a support request: GitHub support form
  • If the App Store listing shows additional support contact details, you can use those as well.

Before You Contact Support

Do not post passwords, Apple ID details, backup files, or sensitive health information in a public support request. Remove personal data from screenshots before sharing them.

Helpful Details To Include

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The app version you are using
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • The exact steps needed to reproduce the issue

App Basics

FitForgeFitness stores workout plans, sessions, profile values, trackers, and settings locally on your device. You can export your data from Settings at any time, and you can clear local app data from inside the app if needed.

Troubleshooting

  • Force close and reopen the app if timers or animations appear stuck.
  • Export a backup before clearing app data.
  • Use the latest iOS version supported by your device.
  • Restart your device if haptics or the system share sheet fail.

Privacy

See the Privacy Policy for details about on-device storage, exports, and data handling.
