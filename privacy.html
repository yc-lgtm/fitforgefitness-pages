FitForgeFitness Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

FitForgeFitness is a local-first fitness app. Core workout data, profile details, plans, streaks, trackers, timers, and settings are intended to remain on your device so the app can function without requiring an online user account.

Information Stored On Your Device

Depending on how you use the app, FitForgeFitness may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Display name, age, height, weight, and fitness level
  • Workout plans, exercise entries, active sessions, and workout history
  • Sleep, food, and jump rope tracking entries
  • Timer presets, streak data, and app preferences

Information Not Routinely Collected By The Developer

FitForgeFitness does not require an account and is not built to routinely upload your workout logs or profile data to a developer-operated backend for core functionality.

  • No in-app account is required
  • No targeted advertising SDK is intentionally included
  • No analytics or tracking SDK is intentionally included for user profiling

When Data May Leave Your Device

Data export or sharing actions occur only when you explicitly request them, such as when you export a backup file or use the system share sheet. Your platform provider or cloud backup provider may also handle device backups according to your own device settings.

Platform And Third-Party Services

FitForgeFitness uses platform-level capabilities for features such as haptics, local file export, and the system share sheet. These support app functionality and are not intended as a developer analytics pipeline.

This public policy page is hosted on GitHub Pages. If you visit this website in a browser, GitHub may process standard website request data under its own policies. That website traffic is separate from the app's core on-device workout tracking behavior.

Data Sharing And Sale

FitForgeFitness does not sell your personal data. Routine app usage is intended to remain local to your device unless you choose to export or share your data.

Your Choices

You can clear local app data from the Settings screen at any time. If you export a backup file, you control where that file is stored or shared.

Children's Privacy

FitForgeFitness does not require an online account and is not designed to intentionally collect personal data from children through a remote backend. Any data entered into the app remains on the device unless it is manually exported or shared.

Contact

For privacy-related questions about FitForgeFitness, visit the Support page or open a request at GitHub support form.
