FitForgeFitness Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

FitForgeFitness is designed as a local-first fitness app. Core workout data, profile details, plans, streaks, and settings are stored on your device.

Data Collection

FitForgeFitness does not require an account to use the app. The app is not designed to collect personal data on a remote server for core functionality.

On-Device Storage

Workout logs, timers, plans, profile values, and preferences are stored locally on your device so the app can function offline and restore your progress.

Data Sharing

FitForgeFitness does not sell your personal data. Export actions are only performed when you explicitly request them.

Your Choices

You can clear local app data from the Settings screen at any time. If you export a backup file, you control where that file is stored or shared.

Contact

For privacy-related questions about FitForgeFitness, use the official support contact associated with the app listing.
